Mission for preventing sexual assaults

Let's get started

Freya is hear for:.

Taking all kind of steps to stop and prevent any type of sexual assaults.

  • Receive complains from victims and take steps accordingly .
  • Giving information to the cops quickly and manage to get to the location on time.
  • Track the location from where the victim gave information and frequently contact the victim to know his/her situation.Victims can also live stream their whole surrounding while they are in the risky area. Freya team will track the location immediately and send alarm to the police.
  • Give support mentally,physically and medically to the victims .
  • Freya team gives education about sexual assault to users by showing educative contents.
  • Freya team also teaches children how to self defence and techniques of getting rid of this situation in order to prevent child sexual abuse.

Freya can give you 24/7 service to support you and help escaping from any kind of sexual crimes. This website has the mission to prevent sexual harassment, rape, groping, child abuse, domestic violence etc.
